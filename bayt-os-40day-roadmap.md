# BAYT OS — بيت — THE 40-DAY FAMILY ROADMAP
## A Strategic Plan for Building the Islamic Family Operating System
### The Seedat Family · Doha, Qatar · Phase 1 of 6

> *"And your Lord has decreed that you worship none but Him, and that you be dutiful to your parents."*
> — Al-Isra 17:23

---

## THE VISION IN ONE SENTENCE

In 40 days, the Seedat family will build a living operating system for their home — rooted in Islam, run like a company, and built *together*, so that every child understands they are not passengers in this family, but co-founders.

---

## HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Every session in this roadmap has five layers:

| Layer | What It Is |
|---|---|
| **WHO LEADS** | The family member driving this session |
| **DURATION** | Realistic time block to protect |
| **LEARN** | What skill, concept, or Islamic principle is embedded |
| **BOND** | The human moment — what makes this session memorable |
| **BUILD** | The tangible output that goes into BAYT OS |

**Session Rhythm:** Monday / Wednesday / Friday — 3 sessions per week.
Friday sessions always run after Jumu'ah. Sunday evenings are Muhammad + spouse (private debrief layer).

**The rule:** Every child-led session, Muhammad's job is to *facilitate, not fix.*
