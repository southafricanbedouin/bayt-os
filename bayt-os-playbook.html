◆ INDIVIDUAL LAYER — THE MOST POWERFUL MEETING
The 1:1 Check-In
One parent. One child. 20 minutes. No agenda except them. This is where the real relationship is built. Logged in Notion.
BI-WEEKLY · PARENT-CHILD
THE RULE
Child's Agenda First

The child always speaks first. The parent's job for the first 10 minutes is to listen — not fix, not advise, not correct. Ask: "What's been on your mind?" Then follow their lead.

THE OUTPUT
Logged. Tracked. Remembered.

Every 1:1 generates a Notion log entry linked to that child's profile. Patterns emerge over time. You begin to see the child more clearly than they see themselves.
