## Purpose of This Shura
This is the **inaugural Family Council** — the first time we formally sit together as a family unit to listen, decide, and commit together. This is not a meeting. It is how we govern our home.

---

## Agenda

### 1. Opening — Bismillah & Intention Setting (5 min)
- Brief du'a together
- State the *niyyah* (intention) of this Shura: we are building something that lasts beyond us

### 2. What Is Family Shura? (10 min)
- Explain the concept to the children in age-appropriate terms
- "Every voice matters. Every voice is heard."
- Establish the ground rules:
  - We listen without interrupting
  - We disagree with kindness
  - What we decide, we honour together

### 3. Running the First Playbook (30 min)
- [ ] Present the Family OS vision — what are we building together?
- [ ] Walk through the Cidly App concept — how will it help us?
- [ ] Share the 3 core pillars: **Faith | Growth | Connection**
- [ ] Each family member shares: *"One thing I want more of in our family"*
- [ ] Each family member shares: *"One thing I want to keep exactly as it is"*

### 4. Family Commitments — Our First Decisions (10 min)
- [ ] Agree on 1 weekly family ritual to protect (e.g., Friday night dinner, Sunday morning)
- [ ] Agree on 1 habit to build together over 30 days
- [ ] Name the family's first shared goal

### 5. Roles & Responsibilities (10 min)
- Who is responsible for what in the family?
- Even young children get a "role" — age-appropriate ownership builds dignity and responsibility
- Document roles here:

| Name | Role | Responsibility |
|------|------|----------------|
|      |      |                |
|      |      |                |
|      |      |                |
|      |      |                |

### 6. Closing — Du'a & Commitment (5 min)
- Close with du'a
- Everyone states one word for how they want to feel in the family
- Schedule **Shura 02** before leaving this meeting
